## Overview

AgriTech AI empowers Kenyan farmers with AI-driven insights for crop management, combining satellite imagery analysis with machine learning to improve yields and reduce losses.

## Key Features

- **Crop Health Monitoring** - AI-powered disease detection from drone and satellite imagery
- **Yield Prediction** - Machine learning models forecast harvest quantities
- **Weather Integration** - Real-time weather data and farming recommendations
- **Market Prices** - Connect farmers directly to buyers with fair pricing

## Impact

Helping 2,000+ smallholder farmers increase yields by 30% through data-driven farming decisions.

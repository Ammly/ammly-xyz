## Overview

M-Pesa Expense Tracker brings intelligent financial management to Kenya's mobile money ecosystem, helping users understand spending patterns and save more effectively.

## Key Features

- **Automatic Categorization** - AI-powered transaction classification for M-Pesa payments
- **Spending Insights** - Visual analytics showing where your money goes
- **Budget Recommendations** - Personalized advice based on spending habits
- **Savings Goals** - Track progress toward financial objectives

## Impact

Helping thousands of Kenyans take control of their mobile money finances with an average of 25% increase in monthly savings.

## Overview

ChurchAdmin AI transforms church media ministries by automating sermon processing, making content more accessible and shareable across digital platforms.

## Key Features

- **Sermon Transcription** - Converts audio/video to accurate text using Whisper AI
- **Smart Summarization** - Generates key points and study guides automatically
- **Content Generation** - Creates social media posts and devotionals from sermons
- **Searchable Archive** - Build a searchable database of all sermon content

## Vision

Empowering churches to extend their reach and impact through AI-powered content transformation and distribution.

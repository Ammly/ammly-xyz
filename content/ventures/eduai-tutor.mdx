## Overview

EduAI Tutor provides personalized, AI-powered education support for Kenyan students, making quality tutoring accessible and affordable through adaptive learning technology.

## Key Features

- **Personalized Learning** - AI adapts to each student's learning pace and style
- **CBC Aligned** - Curriculum matched to Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum
- **24/7 Homework Help** - Instant explanations and step-by-step solutions
- **Progress Tracking** - Detailed analytics for students and parents

## Impact

5,000+ students showing 45% average improvement in grades across STEM subjects.
